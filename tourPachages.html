Available Tours:
Travel to Egypt: Pyramids and Nile by Air
This Egypt holiday package is offered by TheTripBooking among the Egypt tour packages. This tour combines a 5-day spectacular Nile cruise with Cairo's rich past, is a must-have for history lovers. Discover the ultimate Egypt tour, covering all of the country's must-see sites in just eight days, from the majestic pyramids to the abandoned temples dispersed throughout the desert. Cairo is where your tour will start. Here, you will see the Egyptian Museum and the Great Pyramids of Giza. After that, take a plane to Luxor to start your Nile cruise, during which you'll see stunning natural beauty and fascinating attractions between Luxor and Aswan. Enjoy a five-star cruise, a fascinating historical experience, a typical hotel or a boutique hotel, and a sensuous experience with this vacation.
Egypt Trip for Five Days: Cairo and Luxor
Take five days to explore Luxor's and Cairo's attractions. You will arrive in Cairo to begin your journey on private guided tours. This is the site of the famous Sphinx and the Great Pyramids of Giza. Following that, you will take a tour of the Egyptian Museum before heading to Khan El Khalili Bazaar to shop. After that, travel to Luxor in southern Egypt for a tour. You can explore the wonders of the ancient Egyptian culture in Luxor, where you can also view the Luxor Temples, the Valley of the Kings, Karnak, and the Temple of Queen Hatshepsut.
Luxury Tours Charming Egypt and a Tour of the Nile
Take this exclusive, luxurious tour of Egypt and the Nile to see the land of the pharaohs. With this magnificent Cairo and Nile cruise tour, you can see the whole country of Egypt in just eight days. After visiting the Giza Pyramids, Old Cairo, and Saladin's Citadel in Cairo, you will take a Nile cruise from Luxor to Aswan, where you will spend four nights in the ancient sites, including the Temple of Philae, the Valley of Kings, the Karnak Temple, and the Temple of Queen Hatshepsut.
Classic Tours Nile Cruise Round-Trip and Pyramids
With our Round Trip Nile Cruise and Pyramids package, you may treat yourself to the vacation you deserve. On an Egypt vacation, take in the sights of Cairo and spend ten days cruising the Nile. Experience a one-of-a-kind sailing and round-trip journey between Luxor and Aswan for six nights, and visit Karnak Temple, Philae Temple, and many other sites. This 10-day Egypt package vacation includes a view of Cairo's magnificent Pyramids.
