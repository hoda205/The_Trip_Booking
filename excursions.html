Visit to Birqash's Camel Market

24 Hours
Price: $115
Birqash

Excursion Itinerary

Our representatives will pick you up from your accommodation in Cairo or Giza and transport you to Birqash, a small village north of Cairo, to visit the famed Camel Market. You will get a one-of-a-kind experience watching merchants buy and sell camels in market auctions, as well as dealers negotiate and make bargains. People of all socioeconomic backgrounds, as well as dealers, flock there to get relatively cheap and guaranteed healthy meat. Foreign tourists from all over the world visit this well-known market after viewing the Pharaonic structures to see the largest assemblage of camels (the desert ship). Later, you will be transferred from the Camel Market to your hotel.

Scuba diving in Sharm El-Sheikh

24 Hours
Price: $115
Birqash

Excursion Itinerary

Indulge in the thrilling world of scuba diving and discover the enticing mysteries that lie under the surface. Immerse yourself in the ideal introduction to the breathtaking underwater world, where every moment offers enchantment and discovery. Prepare to plunge into the depths of Dive Theory, obtaining a thorough understanding of the underlying life. With expert guidance, embark on a transforming trip that involves a dip in a peaceful swimming pool or a gentle hug in shallow sea waters. Feel the excitement as you dive into the wide waters, reaching a maximum depth of 12 meters and encountering a brilliant tapestry of awe-inspiring aquatic species. Be mesmerized by the colors, the intricate dance of fish schools, and the tranquil elegance of sea animals that will take your breath away.
